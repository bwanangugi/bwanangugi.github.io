    Why you should care

    Data protection affects all of us

    Personal data deserves to be protected

    Data protection laws affect any business, or organisation that collects, processes and holds personal data of individuals. Frankly speaking, that means most organisations- if not all- will be affected.

    Personal data is any information relating to a ‘living’ individual which they can be identified from. Examples of personal data include:

  • Name
  • Passport/Identification Details
  • Postal Address
  • Email Address
  • Date of Birth etc

    • Your organisation might also be processing sensitive personal data which needs to be treated with a higher degree of care due to the significant effects it can have on individuals if this type of data is misused or mishandled.

    Our Services

    How we can serve you

    Data Protection Audits & Gap Analysis

    Data Protection Audits & Gap Analysis (including Kenya + UK GDPR & PECR + EU GDPR + US CCPA).

    An audit is the only way of knowing if your business is compliant. You must have lawful reasons for obtaining and storing personal data, and you must do so in an appropriate legal manner.

    Identify the processing activities in your business that fail to meet the demands of the relevant data protection legislations and asses and strengthen your data protection processes… (and avoid potential fines for non-compliance)!

    Risk Assessments & Remediation

    After delivering our detailed Data Protection GAP Analysis Report, our consultancy team will begin work on the remediation.

    By prioritising your greatest risks - whether this be your outbound marketing activity, your data transfer mechanisms, your data storage capabilities, or policy/contractual failing, our team will establish a working project plan for you based on the report's recommendations.

    This can also help demonstrate to external parties (including the ODPC, if required) that you have committed to a data protection compliance programme to ensure the organisation embraces a “privacy by design” culture

    DPO Services

    We can support your organisation's data protection compliance journey through a number of ways.

  • Reviewing Privacy Policy & Notices
  • Reviewing agreements surrounding data sharing
  • Data Protection vs Marketing Support & Cookie Compliance.
  • Subject Access Request & Rights Support
  • Data Breach Support
  • Data Protection Impact Assessments
  • Creating a Record of Processing Activity

    • Trainings & Workshops

    Workforce training & awareness on data protection matters is crucial for an organisation.Employees who understand data protection for their roles are your greatest asset to remain compliant or identify areas of non-compliance.

    Our various training & workshop sessions enable the development of the requisite knowledge & skills to effectively implement & manage a data protection compliance framework. Our training delivery methods include both on-site (client's office) as well as online virtual training.

    Trainings run from Basic to Intermediary to Advanced sessions that may require a more industry specific focus based on your organisation's needs. We will often provide scenarios that contain questions that should help you understand the core Data Protection Privacy concepts, but also to prepare you for possible assessments at the end of the training.
