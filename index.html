Why BrijX?

Empowering African Users and Businesses!

Enjoy more Choice!

Kwesi works in territory sales for a large African tech multinational. He spends at least a week each every month in Accra, Lagos and Nairobi.

On Brij Mobile, he has seamlessly created three multi-currency wallets - GHS, KES, NGN.

He pays for his Uber airport transfers in all 3 cities with a few clicks and in the local currency in each country. Same goes for his meals from Jumia Food, his hotel bills and the occasional purchases he make at the local supermarket in each city.

Enjoy more Access!

Wavinya has a thriving curio business in one of the largest curio market franchises in the East African region - the Maasai Market.

She has recently gotten interest from buyers based in Lagos who have taken to her beautifully curated curio pieces.

Her customers have struggled with access to Forex previously, losing her quite a bit of business. But now with BrijX, her customers are easily able to settle her in Kenya Shillings, straight from their local Naira wallets!

Enjoy more Convenience!

Kemi and Fola are close sisters who just started their undergraduate studies. While Kemi got admitted to University of Lagos, her sister was accepted at University of Cape Coast in Ghana.

What has made their life some much more enjoyable is the convenience of sending each other funds, and receiving payments for fees, hostels and other school expenses, using the Brij App…all in local currencies of choice!
